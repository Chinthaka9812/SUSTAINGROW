About Us

Independent quality inspection for international buyers.

SUSTAINGROW (PVT) LTD is a Sri Lanka-based independent quality inspection company specializing in coco peat and coir-based growing media. We support international buyers by ensuring products meet required quality standards before shipment.

Our team has deep industry knowledge and hands-on experience in coco peat production. We conduct on-site factory inspections, verify production processes, and perform essential quality checks including EC, pH, moisture, and contamination analysis.

Our Purpose

Reducing risk in the global coco peat market.

Inconsistent quality can lead to shipment rejection, crop damage, and financial loss. Our mission is to eliminate these risks by providing transparent, accurate, and timely inspection services to every buyer we support.
